This will be a Dictionary of Nyaya – (Sanskrit – English) – online and print versions

The format of the dictionary shall be as follows:

1.      Technical Term in Devanagari

2.      Technical Term in roman with diacritical marks

3.      Etymology

4.      derivation according to the Sanskrit language

5.      Detailed Explanation in English

6.      References from texts of Nyaya

7.      Pictorial Illustrations (wherever applicable)

8.      Applicability of the explained concept in the modern context
